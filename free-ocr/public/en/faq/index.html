Frequently Asked Questions

What image formats are supported?
PNG, JPG, and WEBP formats are supported, with a maximum size of 2048×2048 pixels. Larger images will be automatically scaled down.
Is it really free?
Yes, FreeOCR is completely free to use. No hidden fees, no subscriptions, no account registration required. All recognition happens in your browser.
Are my images uploaded to a server?
No. All OCR recognition runs locally in your browser. Images never leave your device. This is FreeOCR's core privacy commitment.
What languages are supported?
Over 100 languages are supported, including English, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, French, German, Spanish, and more.
Can it recognize handwriting?
Yes, handwriting recognition is supported, but accuracy depends on handwriting clarity. Clear, neat handwriting works well; messy scribbles may not produce good results.
Is there a usage limit?
No usage limits. You can use FreeOCR as many times as you want with no daily or monthly quotas.
